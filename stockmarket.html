Overview: This blog post covers the topic of how the stock market works

Two of the most common ways in which companies raise capital are through issuing equity (stocks) and undertaking debts (bonds). In this pose, we will primarily focus on stocks, and the journey they take from a company to an investor through the stock market.

Public companies are most commonly traded on exchanges and over-the-counter transactions.

Exchanges:

Exchanges are locations where investors can buy and sell financial instruments.

How do retail traders interact with an exchange?

Retail traders typically need to go through a brokerage. A broker facilitates transactions between buyers and sellers by placing trades with the exchange on behalf of thier clients.

There are many different types of stock exchanges.
